SNAKE
Rules of the game:
  1. Press any key (from the list below) to start the game.
  2. The snake is light blue and white, the apple is yellow.
  3. Use the keys to direct the snake and pick the apple:
    • Up or W
    • Down or S
    • Left or A
    • Right or D
  4. The game is over if the snake hits the wall or 'eats' itself.
  5. The snake cannot go backward, only forward.
  6. The more apples you collect, the faster the snake moves.
  7. Restart the game when it is over.

* Click the game name to see some cool effects.
